About

The short version

I graduated from Taibah University as a First Honors graduate with a GPA of 4.8, specializing in Security and Networks. In my second year, I placed first in a HackerRank coding competition my professor organized for our Programming 1 class, competing against classmates to solve problems fastest and most efficiently. That competitive edge is what pulled me toward security: I like the puzzle of figuring out where a system is weakest before someone else does.

During my internship at Saudi Paramount Computer Systems, I got to work across the practical spread of cybersecurity: network and firewall configuration, VPN setup, Active Directory administration, and SOC monitoring, reading SIEM alerts and learning to tell real signals from noise.

I'm currently studying for CompTIA Security+, and I'm most energized by SOC operations, network defense, and the kind of detail-oriented analysis that catches what a quick glance would miss.
